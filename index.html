Test Your Maxes
Week 1 is your testing week. Work up to a heavy single on each lift below — this calibrates all your working weights for the entire program.
Testing Protocol
1. Warm up thoroughly — 5–10 min light cardio + dynamic stretching
2. Warm-up sets: 40% × 10, 60% × 5, 75% × 3, 85% × 1, 92% × 1
3. Rest 3–5 min between heavy attempts
4. Work up to the heaviest single rep you can complete with good form
5. Record it below — your program weights update automatically
Don't know your max yet? Leave fields blank — the program will use estimated working weights based on your bodyweight and experience. You can update these anytime.
